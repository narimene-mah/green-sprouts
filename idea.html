Creative Recycling Ideas

Bunny Pencil Holder

Recycle toilet paper rolls into adorable bunny-shaped pencil holders. A fun and easy craft for organizing your desk!

Organized Sorting

Make recycling easy and fun by setting up a colorful sorting station at home. Label bins clearly for paper, plastic, glass, and metal to ensure everything goes to the right place.

Cardboard Creations

Unleash your inner engineer! Old cardboard boxes and bottle caps can be transformed into amazing toys and robots. A perfect rainy day activity for kids and adults alike.
